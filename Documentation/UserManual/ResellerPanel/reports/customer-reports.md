# Customer Reports

Generate reports on customer activity, account status, and growth.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Reports & Export > Customer Reports** from the side menu.

## Available Reports

| Report | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Customer Growth | New customers added per period. |
| Customer Activity | Domains registered, renewed, or transferred per customer. |
| Revenue per Customer | Revenue breakdown by customer. |
| Inactive Customers | Customers with no activity in a specified period. |

## Generating a Report

1. Select the report type.
2. Configure filters (date range, status).
3. Click **Generate**.
4. View results as a table or chart.
5. Optionally export via the [Export](export.md) page.
