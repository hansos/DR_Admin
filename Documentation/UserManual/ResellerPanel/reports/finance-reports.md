# Finance Reports

Generate financial reports including revenue, costs, margins, and tax summaries.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Reports & Export > Finance Reports** from the side menu.

## Available Reports

| Report | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Revenue Summary | Total revenue by period, product, or TLD. |
| Cost Analysis | Registry costs by period and TLD. |
| Margin Report | Profit margins by product, TLD, or customer. |
| Tax Summary | Taxes collected by jurisdiction and period. |
| Invoice Aging | Outstanding invoices grouped by age. |
| Payment Summary | Payments received by method and period. |

## Generating a Report

1. Select the report type.
2. Configure filters (date range, currency, customer, TLD).
3. Click **Generate**.
4. View results as a table or chart.
5. Optionally export via the [Export](export.md) page.
