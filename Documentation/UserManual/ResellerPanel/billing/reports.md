# Reports

Generate billing and financial summary reports.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Billing & Finance > Reports** from the side menu.

## Available Reports

| Report | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Revenue Summary | Total revenue by period, product, or customer. |
| Invoice Aging | Outstanding invoices grouped by age (30/60/90+ days). |
| Payment Summary | Payments received by period and method. |
| Tax Summary | Total taxes collected by jurisdiction and period. |

## Generating a Report

1. Select the report type.
2. Configure filters (date range, customer, currency).
3. Click **Generate**.
4. View the report on screen or export to CSV/PDF.
