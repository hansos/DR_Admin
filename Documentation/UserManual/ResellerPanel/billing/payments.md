# Payments

Record and view payments received from customers.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Billing & Finance > Payments** from the side menu.

## Page Layout

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Payment # | Unique payment identifier. |
| Customer | Customer who made the payment. |
| Invoice | Linked invoice (if applicable). |
| Amount | Payment amount. |
| Currency | Payment currency. |
| Method | Payment method used (e.g., credit card, bank transfer). |
| Date | Date payment was received. |
| Status | Completed, Pending, Refunded. |
| Actions | View, Refund, Link to Invoice. |

## Recording a Manual Payment

1. Click **Record Payment**.
2. Select the customer and invoice.
3. Enter the amount, date, and payment method.
4. Click **Save**. The invoice status updates automatically.

## Automatic Payments

When a [Payment Gateway](payment-gateways.md) is configured, payments are recorded automatically upon successful online transactions.
