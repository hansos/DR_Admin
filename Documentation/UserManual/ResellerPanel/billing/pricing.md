# Pricing

Set and manage pricing for products and services across your reseller account.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Billing & Finance > Pricing** from the side menu.

## Page Layout

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Product | Product or service name (e.g., .com Registration, .no Renewal). |
| TLD | Associated TLD (if applicable). |
| Operation | Registration, Renewal, Transfer, or Service. |
| Cost Price | Your cost from the registry or provider. |
| Sell Price | Price charged to customers. |
| Currency | Pricing currency. |
| Actions | Edit, View History. |

## Setting Prices

1. Click a product row to edit.
2. Enter the sell price for each billing cycle period (1 year, 2 years, etc.).
3. Click **Save**.

## Bulk Pricing

Use **Import/Export** to update prices in bulk via CSV.
