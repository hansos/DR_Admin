# Tax Finalize Tool

Finalize and lock tax calculations for completed orders.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Billing & Finance > Tax Finalize Tool** from the side menu.

## Step-by-Step

1. Select an **Order** or a date range of orders.
2. Review the tax calculations and evidence for each order.
3. Click **Finalize** to lock the tax data.

## What Finalization Does

- Creates an immutable [Order Tax Snapshot](order-tax-snapshots.md) for each finalized order.
- Prevents further changes to the tax calculations for those orders.
- Marks the orders as tax-finalized for reporting and audit purposes.

## Important Notes

- Finalization is irreversible. Verify calculations before proceeding.
- Only orders with status **Completed** can be finalized.
