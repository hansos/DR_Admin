# Pricing per TLD

View and configure registration, renewal, and transfer pricing for each top-level domain.

## How to Access

Navigate to **TLD & Registry > Pricing per TLD** from the side menu.

## Page Layout

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| TLD | Top-level domain. |
| Registration (Cost) | Cost price from the registry. |
| Registration (Sell) | Your sell price to customers. |
| Renewal (Cost) | Registry renewal cost. |
| Renewal (Sell) | Your sell price for renewals. |
| Transfer (Cost) | Registry transfer cost. |
| Transfer (Sell) | Your sell price for transfers. |
| Margin % | Calculated margin percentage. |
| Actions | Edit. |

## Editing Prices

1. Click **Edit** on a TLD row.
2. Adjust sell prices for each operation and period.
3. Click **Save**.
