# Change Password

Update your account password.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Dashboard > Change Password** from the side menu.

## Step-by-Step

1. Enter your **Current Password**.
2. Enter a **New Password**.
3. **Confirm** the new password by typing it again.
4. Click **Save** to apply the change.

## Password Requirements

- Minimum 8 characters.
- At least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one digit.
- At least one special character is recommended.

## Important Notes

- After changing your password you will remain logged in on the current session.
- If you forget your password, use the **Forgot Password** link on the login screen.
