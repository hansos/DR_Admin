# Summary

Overview of your reseller account with key metrics, recent activity, and quick-access widgets.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Dashboard > Summary** from the side menu, or click the **DR Admin** logo.

## Page Layout

### Key Metrics

| Widget | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Active Domains | Total number of domains currently registered under your account. |
| Expiring Soon | Domains expiring within the next 30 days. |
| Pending Transfers | Domain transfers awaiting completion. |
| Unpaid Invoices | Outstanding invoices requiring attention. |

### Recent Activity

A chronological feed showing the latest actions performed in your account (registrations, renewals, transfers, payments).

### Quick-Access Widgets

Shortcut cards to the most common tasks such as **New Quote**, **Domain Lookup**, and **Renew Domains**.

## Tips

- The dashboard refreshes automatically. Use the browser refresh to force an update.
- Click any metric card to jump directly to the corresponding detail page.
