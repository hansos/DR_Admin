# Quick Actions

Shortcuts to frequently used operations like domain lookups and renewals.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Dashboard > Quick Actions** from the side menu.

## Available Quick Actions

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Domain Lookup | Check availability of a domain name. |
| Register Domain | Start a new domain registration. |
| Renew Domain | Quickly renew a specific domain. |
| Transfer Domain | Initiate a domain transfer. |
| Create Invoice | Generate a new invoice for a customer. |
| New Customer | Add a new customer account. |

## How to Use

1. Click the desired quick action card.
2. Fill in the required fields in the dialog or form that appears.
3. Confirm to execute the action.

## Customization

Quick actions displayed here are based on the most common operations. Availability may vary based on your role and permissions.
