# Company Setup

Configure your company name, address, logo, and branding details.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Settings > Company Setup** from the side menu.

## Configuration Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Company Name | Your business name as shown on invoices and communications. |
| Legal Name | Official registered company name. |
| Tax ID | Company tax/VAT registration number. |
| Address | Business address (street, city, postal code, country). |
| Phone | Company phone number. |
| Email | Primary company email. |
| Website | Company website URL. |
| Logo | Upload your company logo (used in invoices, emails, and the panel header). |

## Saving Changes

Click **Save** after modifying any field. Changes take effect immediately on new invoices and communications.
