# Contact Persons

Manage contact persons associated with customer accounts.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Customers > Contact Persons** from the side menu.

## Page Layout

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Name | Full name of the contact person. |
| Customer | The customer account this contact belongs to. |
| Role | Billing, Technical, Administrative, or Primary. |
| Email | Contact email address. |
| Phone | Contact phone number. |
| Actions | View, Edit, Delete. |

## Adding a Contact Person

1. Click **Add Contact**.
2. Select or search for the customer account.
3. Enter the contact details and assign a role.
4. Click **Save**.

## Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Primary | Main point of contact for the customer. |
| Billing | Receives invoices and payment communications. |
| Technical | Receives DNS, hosting, and technical notifications. |
| Administrative | Administrative contact for domain registrations. |
