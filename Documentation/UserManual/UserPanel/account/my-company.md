# My Company

Review and update your company details.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Account > My Company** from the side menu, or select **Edit my company** from the user dropdown in the top bar.

## Page Layout

An editable form with your company and account information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Account Name | Your business or account display name. |
| Contact Person Name | Primary contact person. |
| Email | Primary account email. |
| Billing Email | Email address used for invoices and payment communications. |
| Phone | Contact phone number. |
| Address | Street address, city, postal code, and country. |
| Tax ID | Tax identification number. |
| VAT Number | VAT registration number. |
| Preferred Currency | Default currency for billing. |
| Preferred Payment Method | Default payment instrument for transactions. |

## Saving Changes

Modify any editable field and click **Save** to update your company information. Changes take effect immediately on new invoices and communications.
