# Checkout

Confirm your order, review line items and taxes, and initialize payment.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Shop > Checkout** from the side menu, or click **Proceed to Checkout** from the [Domain Search](domain-search.md) page.

## Page Layout

### Progress Bar

A three-step flow indicator:

1. **Search** — Domain search (completed).
2. **Checkout** — Review and confirm (current step).
3. **Payment** — Payment processing.

### Order Summary

Review all items in your order, including:

- Domain names and their registration period.
- Bundled hosting plans and services.
- Line item prices, applicable taxes, and grand total.

### Actions

| Button            | Description                                                      |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Back to order** | Return to [Domain Search](domain-search.md) to modify the order. |
| **Delete order**  | Discard the order (only if not yet paid).                        |
| **Confirm & Pay** | Proceed to payment using your selected payment method.           |

## What Happens Next

After successful payment, you are redirected to a confirmation page. The domains and services are provisioned automatically.
