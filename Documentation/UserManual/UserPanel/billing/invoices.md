# Invoices

Review your invoice history and payment state.

## How to Access

Navigate to **Billing > Invoices** from the side menu.

## Page Layout

A paginated list of all invoices for your account, showing the most recent first.

### Invoice Summary

Each invoice entry displays:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Invoice # | Unique invoice identifier. |
| Date | Date the invoice was issued. |
| Due Date | Payment deadline. |
| Amount | Total amount due. |
| Status | Paid, Unpaid, Overdue, or Partially Paid. |

### Actions

- **View** — Open the full invoice with line item details and tax breakdown.
- **Download PDF** — Download the invoice as a PDF document.
- **Pay** — Make a payment on an unpaid invoice using your saved payment method.

## Pagination

Use the **Previous** and **Next** buttons to navigate between pages of invoices.
